Breakfast Burrito Recipe

These breakfast burritos will be the perfect morning breakfast to have on the go, just pull them out of the freezer the night before and microwave them in the morning for a quick, nutritious breakfast.

Ingredients

  • 1 Large Sweet Potato, cut into 1/2-inch pieces.
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 6 eggs
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • 1 cup shredded cheese
  • 6 large tortillas
  • 1 can of black beans
  • 1/4 cup cooked chorizo (optional)
  • 1/4 cup salsa verde
  • Hot sauce to serve

Method

  1. Preheat oven to 220c. Spread diced sweet potatos on a baking sheet and drizzle with half the olive oil, toss with salt and pepper to taste
  2. Roast sweet potatos until golden and tender, 15-20 minutes. Let completely cool to room temperature
  3. Heat remaining olive oil in a large pan over medium-low heat. Whisk eggs and milk together and season with salt and pepper. Pour eggs into pan and continuesly stir until eggs start to set. Add cheese and keep stirring till eggs are no longer runny. Remove from heat and let completely cook to room temperature
  4. Wrap tortillas in a dish cloth and microwave for 30 seconds to 1 minute. Assemple wraps with about 1/4 cup sweet potatoes, 1/2 cup scrambled eggs, 1/4 cup black beans and 2 tbps chorizo in a horizontal line across the middle of the tortilla leaving 2 inches on either side. Drizzle wtih salsa verde and fold tortilla to form burrito.
  5. Tightly wrap in plastic wrap or aluminum foil and freeze for up to 1 month or refridgerate for up to 3 days.
  6. To serve remove burrito from plastic or foil and heat on plate in microwave. Microwave 4-5 muntes untll filling is hot and tortilla is tender. Server with hot sauce or salsa.
