Adobo Recipe

Adobo is a philipino dish made usually with chicken or pork served over rice

Ingredients

  • Pork or Chicken
  • Soy Sauce
  • White Vinegar
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Peppercorns
  • Brown Sugar
  • Bay Leaves
  • Green Onion

Method

  1. Mix all ingredients together and let marinade for 2 minutes
  2. Sear the chicken or pork
  3. Let simmer with rest of marinade for 25 minutes until the liquid turns to a glaze
  4. Serve over rice
